Context: a board update email explaining a Q3 product decision

## Paragraph

Our Q3 plan focuses the engineering team on the mid-market segment. The
decision is grounded in two measurements from Q2. First, our 14-day
discovery program (n=42 mid-market prospects across three verticals)
returned 73% qualified-interest at the target price point with a 21-day
median time-to-pilot-decision. Second, the equivalent eight enterprise
discovery conversations returned mixed signal (3 strong-positive, 2
neutral, 3 declined-to-evaluate) — insufficient evidence to redirect
roadmap resources this quarter. The mid-market focus is not a
permanent commitment; we'll re-test enterprise demand in Q1 with a
structured outbound experiment scoped to either confirm or falsify the
Q2 read.
