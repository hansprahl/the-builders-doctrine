## 4. Weekly tripwires per workstream (deliverable #2)

> Tripwires fire at **Thursday EOD week-end check**. The actuals get committed to git the same night as the audit trail. If a tripwire fires, the prescribed action is mechanical — no founder discretion at the gate.

### 4a. Per-workstream tripwires (rows of §1)

| Workstream | Yellow flag (warning) | Red flag (Law VIII fires) | Action on red |
|---|---|---|---|
| EMBA | Hours >20% over forecast | Hours >25% over forecast OR missed class/exam | Pause doctrine for next 48 hrs; protect EMBA |
| Custer | Hours >3/wk for 2 consecutive weeks | Hours >5/wk (campaign ramping into doctrine time) | Pull #10/11/12 forward in slip; revisit re-cut |
| Health | Formations <85% (i.e., missed >2 sessions/wk) | Formations <70% OR missed diabetes/sobriety appt | Hard stop on doctrine work for 1 week; reset baseline |
| Family | Hours >12/wk | Hours >15/wk OR explicit ask from household | Pause non-essential doctrine; protect relationships |
| Other | Hours >12/wk | Hours >15/wk | Audit which "other" commitments to cut |

### 4b. Per-deliverable tripwires

| Stage | Yellow flag | Red flag |
|---|---|---|
| Mid-window | Hours-to-date >60% of estimate | Hours-to-date >100% of estimate |
| Date | — | Missed deadline by 1 day |
| Quality | Founder-romance detector trips (post 05-25) | Two independent reviewer red-flags |

### 4c. Cascade tripwires

1. **#4 statistician not identified by 2026-05-18 EOD** → Replication workstream re-cuts to truncate-at-Platoon (auto-fallback B fires for #5, #11, #12).
2. **Two consecutive yellow flags on any single workstream** → escalate to red. Climb pauses for 1 week.
3. **Any week's actual utilization exceeds 100% of capacity** → climb pauses. Re-cut at the next Thursday gate, not at first missed milestone.
4. **Any single doctrine deliverable misses its re-cut date by ≥25% of estimated hours** → auto-fallback B fires mechanically. Truncate-at-Platoon v1.0 ships. No second re-cut.

### 4d. Weekly check-in protocol (Thursday EOD)

1. Tally actual hours per workstream + per active deliverable for the week.
2. Append actuals to a `BANDWIDTH_ACTUALS_2026.md` log (one row per week).
3. Run §4a + §4b + §4c against the actuals.
4. If any tripwire fires, execute the prescribed action *that night*. No deliberation, no carry-over.
5. Commit the actuals + any triggered action to git as the audit trail.
